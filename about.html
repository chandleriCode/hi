Gas

Gas is a Mythical Elemental-type Blox Fruit that costs $ 3,200,000 or R$ 2,500 from the Blox Fruit Dealer. Gas allows the user to use gaseous (including concentrated and flammable) attacks against their opponents, and will also be granted a purely cosmetic purple, gaseous aura around them. The user will also gain the ability to transform into a powerful gaseous knight, just like the quote above says. Gas has an extremely high AoE (Area of Effect), damage and is an extremely good fruit for combos. Being one of the best tank and anti-teamers of the game due to huge AoE, spammable abilities and overall damage (while transformed) makes it a very good fruit for grinding and fighting. A small downside is that this fruit has a meter which takes some time to fully charge so the user can transform. Its stunning abilities make it able to easily kill high level players with various powerful weapons while not being really good in traditional PvP.

Gas Meter

The user passively gains gas. Additionally, the user gains gas by dealing damage to any NPC/player. In the untransformed state the user cannot lose any of their gas meter. However, in the transformed state, the user receives approximately 45% damage reduction and passively consumes gas, with consumption increasing when taking damage. While transformed, they can also gain more gas meter when dealing damage. When the meter is full, the user unlocks the ability to transform into a gaseous knight. This passive feature is very similar to the original Fury Meter. Gas Meter Elemental Reflex The wielder is immune to all physical attacks (fighting style, sword, gun) from players unless the attacks are imbued with Aura. Against NPCs, this immunity applies only if the player is a certain level above them. This effect is only effective against non-boss enemies. Gas Reflex

Trivia

  • Gas has the same price as Control, with both costing $ 3,200,000 or R$ 2,500.
  • Gas and Control are the second pair of fruits to share a price. the first being Mammoth and T-Rex and the third being Leopard and Yeti.
  • Gas gives a purple aura around the user, similar to Shadow.
  • Gas is the most expensive Elemental fruit in the game as of Update 24.
  • The sound that plays during elemental immunity is the same as the Smoke fruit.
  • Gas is the first and only Elemental fruit that has a transformation.
  • Gas is also the fourth non-beast type fruit to have a transformation, the others being Venom, Rubber, and Diamond.
  • Gas is the second Mythical fruit that is Elemental, the first one being Dough.
  • Gas can infinitely stun Terrorsharks and other difficult bosses such as Cake Queen with its M1s, though a corner is needed for this to work on normal bosses and NPCs.
  • Gas is one of the two fruits that grants a transformation large enough to walk in water without taking damage, the other being awakened Buddha.
  • Gas is regarded as one of the best fruits for Raids, though the M1 for this fruit will severely slow down a Raid if not used correctly.
  • It takes 5 minutes and 30 seconds to fill the Gas Meter without doing any damage.
  • Gas's transformation lasts for 10 minutes (a full day cycle) if the user did not deal any damage to other enemies/players.
  • Gas is one of the three Elemental fruits to not have an awakening, along with Smoke and Blizzard.
  • [V] Vapor Condensation's transformation is the same size as awakened Buddha.
  • Gas is the only elemental fruit to have a meter similar to fury meter.
